DOSE CALCULATIONS

The titration doses were calculated as follows;

@doseTitrationData.numOfBreakthroughDoses breakthrough doses taken over the @doseTitrationData.daysSinceCurrentPrescription days since the last consultation.

Therefore;

Average sum of breakthrough doses per 24 hours
= (number of doses taken x breakthrough dose) / days
= (@doseTitrationData.numOfBreakthroughDoses x @currentPrescriptionData.breakthroughDose mg) / @doseTitrationData.daysSinceCurrentPrescription days
= @doseTitrationData.average24hrBreakthroughDose mg

Average total daily dose
= total daily MR dose + average sum of breakthrough doses per 24 hours
= @doseTitrationData.totalDailyMRDose mg + @doseTitrationData.average24hrBreakthroughDose mg
= @doseTitrationData.averageTotalDailyDose mg
